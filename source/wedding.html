Wedding Event Details

Schedule

  • 9:00 AM - 9:30 AM: Guest Arrival & Seating

  • 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Wedding Ceremony
  • 11:00 AM - 11:30 AM: Vows Exchange & Ring Ceremony
  • 11:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Family Photos & Group Pictures

Afternoon Reception:

  • 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM: Cocktail Hour & Hors d'oeuvres
  • 1:30 PM - 2:30 PM: Reception Dinner

  • 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: First Dance & Open Dance Floor
  • 4:00 PM - 4:30 PM: Cake Cutting Ceremony
  • 4:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Farewell & Send-Off

Speakers

Best Man: John Anderson will share his heartfelt and humorous anecdotes, giving insights into the groom's life and their friendship. John's speech is sure to bring a blend of laughter and tears.

Maid of Honor: Emily Davis, the bride’s closest friend, will deliver a touching speech, sharing the bride’s journey from childhood to this special day. Emily’s words will capture the essence of their friendship and the love story of the couple.

Parents of the Bride & Groom: Mr. and Mrs. Smith and Mr. and Mrs. Johnson will take turns sharing their blessings and well wishes for the newlyweds. Their speeches will reflect the love and support that have guided the couple to this joyous occasion.

Ticket Options

General Admission: Price: $50. Includes access to the wedding ceremony and reception. Guests will enjoy a full day of celebration, including meals and entertainment.

VIP Admission: Price: $100. Includes all General Admission benefits plus priority seating during the ceremony, a complimentary cocktail during the reception, and a special gift from the couple.

Family & Friends Package: Price: $250 (for 4 people). This package offers a group discount for families and close friends, including all General Admission benefits and a reserved table during the reception.
